\problem{}
Let $S_\bullet=\bigoplus_{n\geq0}S_n$ be a graded ring, and for $d\geq1$ let:
\[S^{(d)}_\bullet=\bigoplus_{n\geq0}S_{dn}\]
be the subring generated by homogeneous elements of degree divisible by $d$. Show that the natural inclusion $S^{(d)}_\bullet\subseteq S_\bullet$ induces an isomorphism $\on{Proj}(S_\bullet)\simeq\on{Proj}(S^{(d)}_\bullet)$.

\problem{}
Show that $\PP(1,1,2)$ admits a closed embedding in $\PP^3$.

\problem{}
\begin{enumerate}
 \item Let $X\to\Spec(k)$ be a finite morphism. Show that (the underlying topological space of) $X$ consists of finitely many points with the discrete topology.
 \item Show that finite morphisms have finite fibres.
 \item Find an example of a morphism with finite fibres which is not finite.
\end{enumerate}

\problem{}
\begin{enumerate}
 \item Show that $\on{Proj}(R[t])$ is isomorphic to $\Spec(R)$.
 \item Let $A\subseteq B$ be a finite ring extension. Define a graded ring $S_{\bullet}$ with $S_0=A$ and $S_n=B$ for all $n\geq1$. Show that $\on{Proj}(S_{\bullet})\simeq\Spec(B)$. Show that $S_{\bullet}$ is a finitely generated graded $A$-algebra.
 \item Use the previous point to show that finite morphisms are proper.
\end{enumerate}
