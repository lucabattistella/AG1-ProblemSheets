\problem{}

Let $G(2,4) \subset \PP^5$ be the Grassmannian of lines in $\PP^3$. We denote the homogenoeus Plücker coordinates of $G(2,4)$ in $\PP^5$ by $x_{i,j}$ for $1 \leq i < j \leq 4$. Show:

$$G(2,4) = V(x_{1,2}x_{3,4} - x_{1,3}x_{2,4} + x_{1,4}x_{2,3}).$$

\problem{}
Let $L \subset \PP^3$ be a fixed line. Let $H_L \subset G(2,4)$ be the locus of lines which meet $L$. Consider the incidence correspondence $$I = \{(p,l) \in \PP^3 \times G(2,4) : p \in l\} \subset \PP^3 \times G(2,4).$$ Show that $I$ is a closed subvariety of $\PP^3 \times G(2,4)$. Conclude that $H_L$ is a closed subvariety of $G(2,4)$.


\problem{}
Prove that the Zariski tangent space at the point $L \in G(k,n)$ is canonically isomorphic to $L^* \otimes K^n / L$ (or equivalently to $\Hom(L,K^{n}/L)$).
